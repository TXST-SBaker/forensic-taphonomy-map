About This Resource

Global forensic taphonomy research network · Updated April 2026

About

This platform aggregates publicly available information on all known active human forensic taphonomy research facilities worldwide. These outdoor laboratories study postmortem decomposition across climates — supporting PMI estimation, victim identification, LE training, and disaster response.

How To Use

  • Click any map marker → popup → Full Profile
  • Use ‹ button to collapse sidebar for full-width map
  • Click Stats bar at bottom to expand/collapse it
  • Click 🏷 Legend to toggle the marker key
  • Filter by region using sidebar chips
  • Toggle weather overlays from the toolbar
  • Open a facility panel to see Soil, Climate & Vegetation data
